Mustafa Ghanim and Serhan Gener presented a poster and demo at the 2026 Big Idea Challenge Research 
Showcase as members of the AZSCI team. AZSCI (pronounced "A-Z Sky") aims to complement commercial 
efforts in space-based cloud computing infrastructure, tackling technical challenges alongside safety, 
policy, and business considerations. Please visit [AZSCI](https://research.arizona.edu/news/big-idea-challenge-powers-orbital-data-center-development)
for further details.
